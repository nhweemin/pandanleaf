🎨 How to Use These Icons

  1. Screenshot the main icon above at full resolution (right-click → "Save image as" or use a screenshot tool)
  2. Resize to required dimensions:
    • iOS: 1024×1024px (icon.png)
    • Android: 1024×1024px (adaptive-icon.png)
    • Web: 512×512px (favicon.png)
    • Splash: 400×400px (splash-icon.png)
  3. Replace the files in your assets/images/ folder
  4. Test the app to see your new icon!
